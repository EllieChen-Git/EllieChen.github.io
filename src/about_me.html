About Me

As a coder, I'm a bit different from people in other professions. Instead of counting from 1, I count from 0!

I am a multi-disciplined professional - My professional background is a mixture of languages, accounting and migration law, which allows me to see the world in different perspectives.

I never stop pushing myself to the limit - I completed two 21km half-marathons and a 27km charity walk in Australia, and I went paragliding, glacier hiking and 15,000ft skydiving in New Zealand.

I love engaging with people from different backgrounds - I was backpacking by myself for two years and I travelled to all capital cities in Australia - Darwin, Perth, Adelaide, Melbourne, Hobart, Alice Spring/Uluru, Cairns, Brisbane & the Gold Coast.

I enjoy experiencing different cultures - I have visited Japan, Malaysia, the Netherlands, Belgium, Luxembourg, Norway, UK, Ireland, Germany, France, Singapore, South Korea, Hong Kong, Thailand, USA and Canada.
